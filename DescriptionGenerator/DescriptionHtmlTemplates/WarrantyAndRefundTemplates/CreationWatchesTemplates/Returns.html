• We accept returns and will refund you for your purchase under the conditions outlined below.
  • To be eligible for return or exchange, all watches must be sent back to us within the first 30 days following the receipt of the watch and must meet the following conditions:
    • Watch in original working condition with no accidental damage caused by you
    • No scratches on the watch case, glass or watch strap due to usage
    • No resizing, altering of bracelet
  • If you receive a watch which is damaged in transit or not working on arrival, it is still eligible for return. Please contact us immediately if you received a defective item.
  • Once you return the watch, you can opt for either a refund of the purchase price or an exchange with another watch with the price difference topped up or refunded.
  • We will issue a refund within 24 hours of receipt of the return package.
  • Return shipping charges are the responsibility of the customer. We are not responsible for lost or damaged packages.
